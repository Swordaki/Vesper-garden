## Elijah Roseingrave Graced, Dominion of The Citadel.![[Elijah.jpg]]
## General Description
---
Dominion of the Citadel, father of Wisadel and Izar, husband of Zenith and Maria, and in general; a very strong an proud dragon-man. Remains an older brother figure to [[Kisu]].
## Backstory
---
Son of a man obsessed with power, he was trained brutally for most of his home life an inherited most of his father's lessons while embodying most of his mother's character. Mixing into a new person of his own. When he left his home, a terrible accident in his first world would leave a scar on him that remained open for many years till those he could call friends saved him and made him, him again. He traveled the world with others to find the stardust people an one by one convinced them to become one under one nation with him and his distant cousin, to him almost a sister, as its rulers.
